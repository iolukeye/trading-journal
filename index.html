Entry rules — confirmed
Long entry trigger
Squeeze_Trend_MTF UPTREND on 5m + TrendMagic flipped bullish and held 1+ closed bar → enter on closed bar. Stop at TrendMagic dot at flip bar. 15m must be UPTREND or RANGING.
Short entry sequence — bounce first
Initial break → S.PEAK appears → wait for dead cat bounce → 5m S.CONF on bounce failure → enter short at bounce high. Do NOT enter at initial S.PEAK low.
Hard no-trade conditions
15m RANGING = no trade. VOL:COMP on 15m or higher = no trade. Multi-timeframe COIL stacking = hard session stop.
Chart stack
1000T — execution · 5m — entry confirmer (3:1 nest into 15m) · 15m — trend gate · 1H — context · 30m/1D — structure
Indicator hierarchy
Market Phase → Volume → TTM Wave → Delta Pressure
Daily rules
Hard daily stop
$250 loss — absolute. No exceptions.
TopstepX cap: No single day > ~$1,500 net.
Trade sizing
Grade A = full size. Grade B = 50%, faster exit. ETH: 1–2 MNQ. RTH: 2–3 MNQ.
Execution mistakes — know these cold
⚠ Entering short at initial S.PEAK low — wait for dead cat bounce.
⚠ Exiting valid trends on pullbacks — wait for confirmed TrendMagic flip.
⚠ B.PEAK at known HTF resistance = full exit, not hold.
⚠ S.CONF during STABLE uptrend = pullback noise, not reversal.
